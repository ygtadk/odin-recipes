Lentil Soup

About This Recipe

  • How Many Servings: 6 servings
  • Preparation Time: 10 minutes
  • Cooking Time: 50 minutes
  • Calories Per Serving: 290 kcal

Ingredients

  • 3 tablespoons of sunflower oil
  • 1 onion (chopped coarsely)
  • 1 tablespoon of flour
  • 1 carrot (chopped coarsely)
  • 1 potato (large, coarsely chopped)
  • 1 dessert spoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon of black pepper
  • 1.5 cups of red or yellow lentils
  • 6 cups of hot water (preferably prepared with broth)

For the Sauce

  • 3 tablespoons of oil
  • 2 spoonful butter
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder

How to Make Lentil Soup Recipe?

  1. Add 3 tablespoons of oil to a deep pan. Fry 1 large chopped onion with vegetable oil.
  2. Add 1 tablespoon of flour to the roasted onions and continue the roasting process until the smell comes out and turns color. Transfer one carrot and one potato, which you cut into large pieces, into the pot and continue mixing.
  3. After washing with salt, pepper and plenty of water, add 1.5 cups of drained lentils and mix well for the last time.
  4. Add 6 cups of hot water to the pot.
  5. Then close the lid and cook for 40 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the potatoes and carrots are soft.
  6. In order for the soup to get a smooth consistency after cooking; through a hand blender. After cooking for another 5 minutes, remove from the stove.
  7. Heat 3 tablespoons of oil and 2 tablespoons of butter in a pan. Add 1 dessert spoon sweet red pepper powder and heat the oil for 2 minutes, then remove from the stove.
  8. Take the soup in a bowl and pour the hot sauce on it and serve.
