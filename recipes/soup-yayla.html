Yayla (Wold) Soup

About This Recipe

  • How Many Servings: 6 servings
  • Preparation Time: 15 minutes
  • Cooking Time: 35 minutes
  • Calories Per Serving: 190 kcal

Ingredients

  • 2 glasses of yogurt
  • 2 tablespoons of butter
  • 1.5 tablespoons (heaped) flour
  • 2 teaspoons of salt
  • 2 tablespoons of lemon juice
  • 1 egg yolk
  • 1 teaspoon of paprika
  • 6 glasses of water
  • 1 teaspoon of rice
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon of dried mint

How to Make Yayla (Wold) Soup Recipe?

  1. Add 2 glasses of yoghurt, 1.5 tablespoons of flour, 2 teaspoons of salt and 2 tablespoons of lemon juice to the pot where you will cook the soup and mix until it reaches a smooth consistency.
  2. Add 1 egg yolk to the mixture and mix again.
  3. Add 6 glasses of cold water and let it boil over medium heat.
  4. When the soup starts to boil, add 1 tea glass of rice that you washed and strained and let it boil again.
  5. Meanwhile, heat 2 tablespoons of olive oil and butter in a separate pan. Add 1 tablespoon of dried mint, mix and remove from the stove.
  6. Pour the prepared oil over the boiling soup.
  7. Mix well and turn off the stove.
  8. Your Yayla soup is ready, bon appetit!
